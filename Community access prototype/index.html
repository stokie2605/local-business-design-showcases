How the foodbank works

A smooth journey from local generosity to dignified support.

Move through the steps to see how donations, volunteers, secure Save Our Supper referrals, and careful local delivery work together.

Step 1

Local Donations

Residents bring everyday staples into the local network through familiar community collection points and church partners. Grouped drop-off points make it easy to give while shopping, visiting church, or using a local service.

Church collection points, donation hampers, and local baskets across Alsager

Step 2

Volunteer Sorting

Volunteers check dates, organise shelves, prepare stock, and keep donations moving safely. It is careful, steady work that turns many small gifts into reliable local support.

Operational base

The Annexe, Lawton Road

Local coordination point for sorting, packing, and arranging support.

Our Operational Base — Located at The Annexe, to the rear of Alsager Community Church, Lawton Road.

Step 3

Secure Referrals

Trusted agencies submit food parcel requests through the Save Our Supper digital system, giving the foodbank team the right information quickly and securely without asking people to repeat their story.

Secure referrals connect trusted local agencies with the foodbank base

Step 4

Dignified Delivery

Parcels are prepared with care, matched to household needs where possible, and arranged for local collection or delivery with privacy, respect, and complete confidentiality.

Milton Park gardens and local pathways reflecting quiet, respectful support

Referral process

A secure bridge into Save Our Supper.

You do not need to explain everything twice. A trusted local professional can make a secure referral, and the foodbank team will help with care and discretion.

Agency Portal: Submit a Food Parcel Request
1

Speak to a Trusted Professional

GPs, schools, Citizens Advice, Social Services, and other front-line teams can help begin the request.

2

Secure Digital Referral

The professional submits your request instantly through the secure Save Our Supper platform so the right details reach the foodbank team clearly.

3

Care & Discretion

The foodbank team prepares your parcel for local collection or delivery with complete confidentiality.
